Projects

Census Data Analysis

During the 2020 summer, along with 2 of my peers, I worked on a project that aims to predict whether a person with a given set of characteristics and statistics will make above or below $50,000. Some of the data that we were provided with in our dataset were the person's race, sex, marital status, occupation and whether they made more than, or less than or equal to $50K. Using this data we fitted many types of models that we learned and were able to achieve around 76% accuracy. We were hesitant in putting characteristics such as race and sex in while we trained our model because we did not want to make our model bias to any discrimination. See more details in our presentation and see my GitHub for the code.

Energy Consumption Data Analysis

With the same group of peers, we also started a second project for predicting how much energy is likely to be consumed given the date and time.
